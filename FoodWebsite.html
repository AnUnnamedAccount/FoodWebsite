About Us

John & James:

Motto: Real Italian Delights

John & James is a chain of Italian restaurants in the Jakarta, Indonesia area, with locations in places like Graha Raya, BSD, and Green Lake City.

The restaurants are known for serving authentic Italian cuisine, including pizza and pasta,

and are popular for their friendly atmosphere and delicious food.

Some locations also cater to vegans and offer a separate vegan menu with options like vegan pizza, pasta, and gnocchi.
